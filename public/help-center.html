FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I place an order?
Browse our shop, click "Add to Cart" on any product you want, then open your cart and click "Checkout Now." You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase. Fill in your shipping details, review your order, and confirm.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number. Log in to your account and go to your Dashboard to view your orders and tracking information.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure checkout system powered by Stripe. This includes Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
Can I change or cancel my order?
If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact us as soon as possible and we'll do our best to make changes or cancel it. Once an order has shipped, we cannot make changes but you can initiate a return once you receive it.
How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar. Fill in your first name, last name, email, and choose a password. Once registered, you can manage your orders, save addresses, and check out faster.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot your password?" Enter your email address and we'll generate a password reset token for you. Use that token to set a new password.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, and Australia. Shipping times and costs vary by location. Check our Shipping Info page for details.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Visit our Returns page for the full policy.
